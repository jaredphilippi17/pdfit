Frequently Asked Questions

Where are my documents stored?

All PDFs are stored locally on your device. PDFit does not upload your files to cloud servers.

Does PDFit require a subscription?

No. PDFit is designed as a one-time purchase with no recurring subscription.

Does PDFit contain advertisements?

No. PDFit is completely ad-free.

Does OCR require an internet connection?

No. OCR is performed on your device using Google ML Kit.
