Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Overview

PDFit ("the app") is designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share your personal data.

Data Storage

All documents you scan or import are stored locally on your device. We do not upload your files to any external servers.

Data Collection

PDFit does not collect personal information, analytics data, or usage tracking.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Google ML Kit for OCR processing. Text recognition happens locally on your device and is not sent to external servers by PDFit.

Security

You may optionally enable biometric authentication or device PIN protection. This feature uses Android system security and does not transmit biometric data anywhere.

Children’s Privacy

PDFit does not knowingly collect data from children or any users.

This privacy policy may be updated in the future. Changes will be posted on this page.
